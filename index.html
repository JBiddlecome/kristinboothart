About Kristin

Painter, colorist, storyteller.

Kristin Booth blends bold chroma with subtle texture, building layers that invite viewers to pause, breathe, and feel. Each piece is influenced by coastal light, memory fragments, and the way sound translates into color on canvas.

Her work lives in private collections and intimate public spaces. Between commissions, she experiments with textile translations so collectors can live with the work in new ways.
